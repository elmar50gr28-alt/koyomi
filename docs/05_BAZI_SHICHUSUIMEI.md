# Bazi / Shichusuimei Specification

## Required Person Data

- Birth date
- Birth time
- Birthplace

Name is not required for Bazi/Shichusuimei alone.

## Related East Asian Systems

### Sukuyo

Required:

- Birth date

Conditional:

- Birthplace and birth time zone
- Birth time near day boundary

### Nine Star Ki

Required:

- Birth date
- Target date/time
- Reference location
- Destination
- Direction purpose
- School setting

## Conditional Data

- Birth time unknown
- Time accuracy
- Latitude and longitude
- Time zone at birth
- True solar time
- Zi hour 23:00/00:00 setting
- Gender and school setting when needed for luck-cycle direction

## Output Requirements

- Show pillars and calculation basis.
- Show school settings.
- Show confidence when time or location is partial.
- Explain when a reading is limited by missing time.

## AI Boundary

AI may explain the calculated result but must not change pillars, luck cycles, or strength judgments.
